HMCScan - Boxes Locations

This window is designed to define Boxes Locations.


Step 1
Select in the list Boxes to be updated with the Location Name.

Step 2
Give the Name of the Location, and then click the button [Update].

Step 3
Repeat these operations for others Boxes and Locations.

Step 4
Click the [Ok] button to finish and update Boxes Locations in the Database.
