List of Customers

This window shows two lists of Customers :
  • The left list displays the list of all the Customers defined in SACR.
    This list is mainly stored on an IBM Web Server (Global List).
    You can filter this list by the "Enterprise Name" if necessary.

  • The right list displays your Favorite Customers.

Button [ -> ]
Click on this button to select a Customer from the left list and add it on your Favorite List in the right.
You can obtain the same result with a double click on the left list.

Button [ <- ]
Click on this button to remove a Customer from your Favorite List.
You can obtain the same result with a double click on the right list.

Button [Reload]
If a new Customer is added on the Global List by the Administrator, you just have to click on this button to download the new list on your computer.

Button [View Details]
Click on this button to display the Account used for the selected Customer.

Button [Quit]
Click on this button to return to the previous screen.
