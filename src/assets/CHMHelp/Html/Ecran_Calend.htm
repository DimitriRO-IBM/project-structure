Calendar

This window allows you to select a date. First select the Month and the Year in the lists.
You can also use the buttons and to increase or decrease the calendar of a month.

Days are displayed in the calendar : previous month, selected month and next month.
Then click on the selected day, in one of the 3 displayed months. To validate your selection, click on the button [Ok] or double click directly on the selected day.

Button [Actual Day]
Click on this button to select the actual day.

Button [Cancel]
Click on this button to cancel your selection.
