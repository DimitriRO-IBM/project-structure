How to generate the "Pages Breaks" Manually

Follow these steps in the Word document :
  • Launch the Word command "Replace" (Ctrl+H)
  • In the field "Find What", type [PB]
  • In the field "Replace with", type ^m or :
    - Click on the [More >>] button
    - Click on the [Special] button
    - Select "Manual Page Break"

    You obtain these :



    - Click the [Replace All] button
